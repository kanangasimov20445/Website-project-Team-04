THE HOUR OF CODE

ABOUT US

Our goal is to encourage young children in computer science and engineering through the "Hour of Code" event. In addition, not only computer science, but also numerical sciences and creativity are among our most important priorities. That's why we go to schools and teach children about algorithms and computer science and try to orient them to this science, even if it is basic.
